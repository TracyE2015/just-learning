Next Generation

Our next generation cruisers and destroyers are the backbone of the fleet and are multi-mission purpose vessels. All of our cruisers and destroyers have been proven to be the most versatile starships that the Hyperion Planetia Shipyard has built because they are faster and more powerful and contain state of the art technology for space travel.


With these starships the bridge is centered on the top of the primary hull and navigation dome is centered underneath. The original series starships has two phaser banks whereas the next generation starships are equipped with ten phaser banks which are mounted radially on the top and the bottom of the primary hull and secondary hull and also equipped with multiphasic shielding. These starships are truly formidable.
