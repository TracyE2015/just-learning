New Series

Our new series of starships consist of the Dromonis Class Excelsior Battlecruiser, Gallirus Rothwell Class Light Cruiser and the new Kronodon Class Solaris Cruiser.


Dromanis Class Excelsior Battlecruiser


This battlecruiser is one of our well armed, general purpose starship. It is a defense capable vessel. We added hull reinforcements on both sides of the navigation deflector to help decrease damage in light battles.


Gallirus Class Rothwell Light Cruiser


This light cruiser is lightly armed, general purpose starship. It is extremely maneuverable due to its high power to mass ratio.


Kronodon Class Solaris Cruiser


This cruiser is the fastest starship we ever built. Please see Kronodon Class Solaris Cruiser to read more about it.
